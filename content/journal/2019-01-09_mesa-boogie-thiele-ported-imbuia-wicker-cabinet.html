This is the Holy Grail of Mesa cabs, a vintage hardwood & wicker Thiele with EVM-12L from the Mark III era. When you consider this thing is around 30 years old it is in pretty nice shape. It was made using amazing 13" wide imbuia boards. Condition is pretty nice overall. There are dents and dings throughout, and also some substantial scratches on top from amp feet. The speaker and it has a 1989 date code.

Weight is 47lbs.
