Swamp Ash SF3 Lightweight Guitar

Posted on December 4, 2005 by Scott French

"This is a project that evolved a lot from start to finish, but one goal all along was to make a very light guitar. This one-piece swamp ash body was light to start, but I had the face sliced off so we could do some chambering and make it even lighter."

BODY
SF1

FINISH
Polyester

WEIGHT
~5 lbs

Body

One Piece Swamp Ash Body
Dark Pore Filler
Roundover/Comfort Carved Top

Neck

25" Scale Length
5-Piece Flame Maple/Wenge Neck
Pau Ferro Fingerboard
2mm Abalone Side Dots
Swamp Ash/Black Lam Headcap/Healcap
24 Jumbo/Tall Stainless Steel Frets
Gloss Polyester Neck Finish
Standard/Round 'C' Neck Profile
2 Way Adjustable Truss Rod
1 11/16" Black Corian Nut

Hardware

Chrome Hardware Finish
Hipshot Baby Grand Bridge
Planet Waves Locking Auto Trim Tuners

Electronics

2 Jason Lollar P90s
Volume/3-way/Tone
