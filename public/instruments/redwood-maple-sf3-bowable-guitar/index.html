Redwood & Maple SF3 Bowable Guitar

Posted on December 16, 2007 by Scott French

"This SF3 came with a fairly straightforward goal: to be easy to play with a bow. Narrowing the SF3 design and doing an exagerated top carve ended up being the easy part... findings pickups and a bridge to accomodate that extreme radius was more challenging. Nordstrand was able to supply some custom NDC humbuckers with super staggered magnets for the pickups. For the bridge I used Graphtech saddles and built a very custom ebony bridge by hand."
