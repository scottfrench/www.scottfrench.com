Pink Paisley SF3 Leo Fender Tribute Guitar

Posted on September 25, 2008 by Scott French

"I built this guitar as a tribute to Leo Fender. Like every gutiar builder ever I love his material choices, simple solutions, and timeless designs. I didn't want to lean all the way into the vintage trap though so I included some crazy 18v active pickups. If I built this one again I would do an oiled & waxed bolt-on neck."
