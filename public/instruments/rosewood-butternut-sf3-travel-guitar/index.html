Bolivian Rosewood & Butternut SF3 Travel Guitar

Posted on December 4, 2006 by Scott French

"I don't have any stats to back this up, but I am fairly certain this was my most popular guitar. I still get random emails from people looking to pick one up. I'm really happy with the design and how the guitar turned out, but the Steinberger tuners are a little wonky so I was always considering different options for future travel guitars."

BODY
SF3 Travel

FINISH
Polyester

BAG
Cocaine Mule

Body

Bolivian Rosewood Top over Butternut Body
Top & Back Roundover w/Comfort & Belly Carves

Neck

25.5" Scale Length
5 Piece Birdseye Maple/Wenge Neck
Acrylizied Birdseye Maple Fingerboard
2mm Abalone Side Dots
24 Super Jumbo Bell Brass Alloy Frets
Thick/Fat Neck Profile
2 Way Adjustable Truss Rod
1 5/8" Throat Width

Hardware

Gold Hardware Finish
Birdseye Maple Pickup Covers
Bolivian Rosewood Pickup Rings
Schaller Locking Strap Buttons
Steinberger Gearless Tuners w/Schaller Roller Bridge

Electronics

Nordstrand NDC Humbuckers
Volume with On/Off Switch for each Coil
