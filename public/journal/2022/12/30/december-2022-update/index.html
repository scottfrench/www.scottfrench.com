December 2022 Update

Posted on December 30, 2022 by Scott French

The last few months of the year are always busy around here. Holidays, birthdays, anniversaries, school events, and end-of-year work always means there is no free time. It's been a great and full year for my family so I have no complaints, but I have not been able to touch anything guitar related in some time. I'm coming into the New Year with even more focus than last year, so I'm excited for the coming months. I had three main guitar goals for 2022, and while I was only able to complete one of them, I did make progress on all three. I'm looking forward to taking that momentum into 2023. I will finish a new guitar this year, and hopefully even more than that.

Thanks very much to everyone following along and helping push me forward. I appreciate hearing from people via email even though I am often not the fastest to respond. Keep your messages coming because I really love hearing from folks from all over the world and discussing luthiery with everyone. I wish you all a wonderful, productive, and fulfilling 2023!

One new thing for me this year, I plan on attending the 2023 GAL Convention in Washington. It has been many years since I went to any events so I am looking forward to a long weekend hanging out with guitar people. Please reach out if you are planning on attending so we can connect and say hello.
